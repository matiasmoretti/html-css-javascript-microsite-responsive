We are delicious!!!!

Located in Toronto, Georgian Pizza was originally a small bar where you could find pool tables and patrons playing cards. Italian immigrant workers would come to relax and enjoy pizza with their families to remember the taste of their motherland.

Today we are known for our simplicity and the authenticity of our recipes and also offer the delivery service, especially to the coldest dates. Pizza is a passion for Canadians and for us, this passion has grown over the years and throughout generations. We are made from history and continue doing history integrating traditional Canadian flavors and innovative services to our clientele.


In pizza we trust!
